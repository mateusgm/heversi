Kram Rules

In Kram, there are two players who must place rectangular pieces on the board. One of the players may only place them horizontally, while the other player must place the pieces vertically. Each piece is the size of two squares on the board. The object of the game is to place more pieces than your opponent. To place a piece, click on the square where you want the left or upper half of your piece to be.
